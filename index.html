My Recipies

Cool and creamy, sorbets are the ultimate light summer treat. They're not only super refreshing (and palate cleansing), but are also some of the easiest frozen desserts to make. Since most sorbets do not contain any dairy (with a few rare exceptions), they make a delightful alternative to ice cream for the dairy-free folks in your group.
